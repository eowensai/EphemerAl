## 2025-10-26 - [High Contrast Hover States & Destructive Action Clarity]
**Learning:**
1. The brand accent color (#E1654A) has poor contrast (2.95:1) with white text.
2. Destructive actions like "New Conversation" benefit from explicit tooltips to prevent accidental data loss (session clearing).
**Action:**
1. Use the dark text color (#1E242B) on hover states to ensure accessibility (5.8:1).
2. Add explanatory tooltips to session-clearing buttons.
