## 2024-05-23 - Material Icons vs Emojis in Streamlit
**Learning:** This project explicitly prefers Streamlit's Material icons (e.g. `:material/refresh:`) over Unicode emojis for standard buttons to maintain a professional, cohesive aesthetic. Emojis can look informal and vary wildly across platforms.
**Action:** When adding new buttons, check Streamlit documentation for available Material icons instead of reaching for the emoji picker.
